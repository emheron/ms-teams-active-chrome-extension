Wiggle Teams

Keeping you active on Microsoft Teams

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Wiggle Teams Extension This extension does not collect or share personal information. The only function of the Wiggle Teams extension is to interact with the Microsoft Teams website to prevent your status from being shown as idle. It does not track, store, or transmit personal data, nor does it include any analytics or tracking software. The extension does not have any form of communication with servers, databases, or outside services. All functionality is self-contained within your browser. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.
