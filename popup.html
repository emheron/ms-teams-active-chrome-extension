Wiggle Teams

Keeping you active on Microsoft Teams

Status: Inactive

How to Use

  1. Install the extension by following the installation instructions provided with the package.
  2. Open Microsoft Teams in a browser tab.
  3. The extension will automatically keep your status active by simulating user actions at intervals of approximately 10 seconds.
  4. Important: Ensure that Microsoft Teams is not open simultaneously in multiple browsers or with the desktop app for optimal functionality. Having Teams open in multiple places may impact the functionality of this extension.
  5. If you need to stop the extension, simply close the Microsoft Teams tab or disable the extension through your browser's extension settings.

Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect or share personal information. The only function of the Wiggle Teams extension is to interact with the Microsoft Teams website to prevent your status from being shown as idle. It does not track, store, or transmit personal data, nor does it include any analytics or tracking software. The extension does not have any form of communication with servers, databases, or outside services. All functionality is self-contained within your browser. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.
